Welcome to FindMyFamily

Always know about your loved ones' whereabouts. Live worry-free.

FindMyFamily is an open-source alternative to popular group-location tracking applications like Life360 Family Locator and Glympse.


Features:
  • Real-time Location Tracking
  • Add, remove members from the family group
  • Easy authorization with Google OAuth
  • Add upto 16 members in a group

  • Coming Soon:

    Share location information with contacts

  • Coming Soon:

    Chat, connect with group members in the app
